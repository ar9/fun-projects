You will receive one TOON object called GAME_DATA. It contains 2025 team statistics, rosters, depth charts, injury information, betting odds, ESPN matchup predictor values, weather, home-field context, rivalry notes, and volatility indicators.

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Parse GAME_DATA silently.

2. Use only the information provided inside GAME_DATA and generic football knowledge about how stats and injuries translate into outcomes. Do not add outside data or assumptions.

3. Interpret all fields correctly:
   - Treat betting odds and ESPN matchup predictor as important calibration signals.
   - If the point spread is within plus or minus three points, treat the matchup as close. In a close matchup, projected scoring margins should usually fall within three to seven points, and confidence_winner must stay between 0.50 and 0.65.
   - If external predictors disagree with season stats, lower your confidence and expand the risk factors.
   - Major injuries, especially OUT or doubtful designations for key players, must materially affect your prediction and confidence.

4. Predict the following:
   - winner_team_id
   - winner_team_name
   - projected_score for both teams
   - confidence_winner on a 0 to 1 scale
   - confidence_score_band as either low, medium, or high

5. Provide a concise explanation:
   - Give three to five key factors that support your predicted winner.
   - Give two to four risk factors that could cause your prediction to fail.
   - All factors must be grounded directly in specific fields in GAME_DATA such as stats, injuries, depth chart issues, betting odds, or external predictor values.

6. Do not:
   - Add stats not present in GAME_DATA.
   - Use historical rivalry narratives unless they are explicitly included in GAME_DATA.
   - Refer to your chain of thought or describe how you reasoned.
   - Output any prose outside the JSON object.
   - Use code fences or backticks.
   - Add commentary or disclaimers.

7. Output format:
Return only a valid JSON object with this exact structure:

{
  "winner_team_id": 1,
  "winner_team_name": "Texas",
  "projected_score": {
    "1": 27,

Do not include anything before or after the JSON.
